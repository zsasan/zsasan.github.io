Hello, I'm Zeinab Sasan.

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Engineering (Computer Networks) under the supervision of Dr. Khorsandi at Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic). I received my M.Sc. in Computer Engineering (Networks) from Sharif University of Technology and my B.Sc. in Information Technology Engineering from University of Tabriz.

Since 2015, I have been actively involved in various industrial projects related to computer networks and network security. My work focuses on designing efficient, secure, and scalable systems that bridge academic research and practical applications in telecommunication networks.
